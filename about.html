About FeedingUs

Our Mission

FeedingUs is a platform that bridges the gap between restaurants and charity organizations, fostering a seamless connection to combat food waste and hunger. We empower restaurants to donate surplus food and enable charities to efficiently distribute it to communities in need.

How We Work

Our platform acts as a digital intermediary, providing tools and services to streamline donations, track contributions, and ensure transparency. By connecting restaurants with verified charities, we create a network that maximizes impact and minimizes waste.

Our Services

Restaurant Dashboard

Restaurants can manage their menus, track surplus food, schedule donations, and view analytics to optimize their contributions. The dashboard provides real-time insights and simplifies the donation process.

Charity Dashboard

Charities can browse available donations, coordinate pickups, track received contributions, and connect with local restaurants. The dashboard ensures efficient distribution to those in need.

Admin Portal

Our admin portal allows platform administrators to oversee all activities, verify users, manage disputes, and ensure smooth operations across the network.

Notification Service

Stay informed with real-time notifications about donation statuses, new opportunities, and platform updates. Our system keeps all users connected and engaged.

Authentication Service

Secure user authentication ensures that only verified restaurants and charities access the platform, maintaining trust and safety.

Donation Management

Our donation management system tracks every step of the donation process, from scheduling to delivery, providing transparency and accountability.
